# Google Play Store Listing — AntiTheft Alarm

## App Details
- **App name:** AntiTheft Alarm
- **Short description (80 chars):** Intelligent theft alarm that protects your phone when charging or unattended.
- **Full description:**
  AntiTheft Alarm turns your Android device into a smart theft deterrent. Designed for phones left charging in public places — cafes, airports, libraries, and shared workspaces.

  HOW IT WORKS
  Set a secure app-level PIN, arm Guard Mode, and walk away. If someone disconnects your charger or moves your phone, a loud alarm sounds instantly. The alarm plays at maximum volume, overrides silent mode, and works even when the screen is off.

  FEATURES
  • Charging Guard — alarm triggers if power is disconnected while armed
  • Motion Guard — detects movement via the accelerometer (coming soon)
  • SIM Guard — detects SIM card removal or swap (coming soon)
  • Intruder Selfie — captures a front camera photo after failed PIN attempts (coming soon)
  • Geofence Guard — alerts when you leave a safe zone (coming soon)

  WHY ANTITHEFT ALARM
  • No account required — everything stays on your device
  • No ads, no tracking, no permissions misused
  • Open source and completely free

  IMPORTANT: This app uses the Device Admin API and Notification Listener Service for theft detection features.

## Privacy Policy
This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data (PIN, settings) is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. No internet connection is required. The app uses the Camera only for the Intruder Selfie feature, which is stored locally and never uploaded unless you explicitly share it.

## GDPR / Data Safety
- Data shared: None
- Data collected: None
- Data encrypted: All local data (PIN) is encrypted
- Data deleted: Clear app data or uninstall to remove all data
