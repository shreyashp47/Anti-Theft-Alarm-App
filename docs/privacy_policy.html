Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

AntiTheft Alarm is an anti-theft alarm application developed to run entirely on your Android device. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your data (PIN, settings, event history, intruder photos) is stored locally on your device using secure Android mechanisms like EncryptedSharedPreferences and internal storage.

2. Permissions Used

  • Notifications: Required for the persistent foreground service notification on Android 8+.
  • Location: Used for Geofence Guard feature (optional).
  • Camera: Used for Intruder Selfie feature (optional, locally stored).
  • Phone State: Used for SIM Guard feature (optional).
  • Device Admin: Used for anti-disable protection (optional).
  • Display over other apps: Shows alarm screen when phone is locked.
  • Battery optimization exemption: Prevents system from killing the alarm service.

All permissions are optional. Revoking a permission simply disables the related feature.

3. Children's Privacy

This app is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties. There are no ads, analytics, or external SDKs that track or transmit data. The only optional data transmission is SMS or email alerts to a trusted contact that you explicitly configure.

5. Open Source

This app is fully open source. The complete source code is available on GitHub for independent verification.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any future changes will be updated in this document and reflected in the GitHub repository.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, you can reach out via the GitHub repository.

Thank you for using AntiTheft Alarm.
